The mechanics, honestly

  • 30% flat, lifetime-recurring. Attribution is stored on the first payment event and looked up on every renewal — that's what makes it lifetime, not first-invoice-only.
  • Idempotent ledger. Every commission carries a unique event id; a re-delivered webhook never double-pays.
  • Refunds claw back. A refund inserts a negative entry against the same subscription, so the ledger nets out. We'd rather you trust the report than be surprised by it.

Honest limits, for your audience: gate.cat is a deterministic wall in front of known-dangerous command shapes — not a proof of safety. An unmatched action is unchecked, not safe.
